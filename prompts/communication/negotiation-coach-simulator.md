# Negotiation Coach Simulator (RU)

## Purpose

A complex AI prompt for running fully customized negotiation simulations with an adaptive AI agent.

The prompt creates a dual-role AI:
- a professional negotiation coach,
- and a role-based opponent simulator ("Chameleon") capable of imitating behavioral patterns or well-known public figures.

The goal is not entertainment, but realistic skill training and structured feedback.

---

## When to use

This prompt is suitable when you need to:
- practice negotiations without real-world risk,
- train against different negotiation styles and pressure levels,
- receive structured, personalized feedback after the session,
- experiment with difficult or high-stakes negotiation scenarios.

Typical use cases:
- business negotiations,
- stakeholder communication,
- salary or contract discussions,
- conflict-heavy or asymmetric negotiations.

---

## Key features

- Three-phase interaction model: briefing ‚Üí simulation ‚Üí analysis.
- Strict role separation between coach and opponent.
- Adaptive difficulty and personalization.
- Detailed post-session analytical report with a success/failure verdict.
- Support for role imitation (public figures or described behavioral styles).

---

## Language note

The prompt is written in **Russian**.

This is intentional, as negotiation training strongly depends on linguistic nuance, tone, and cultural context.

---
