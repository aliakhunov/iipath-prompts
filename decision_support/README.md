# Decision support prompts

AI prompts designed to support decision-making through critical analysis, skepticism, and structured reasoning.

This directory focuses on reducing decision risk by questioning assumptions, identifying weaknesses, and exposing blind spots before actions are taken.

---

## Purpose

Prompts in this directory are intended to:
- critically evaluate ideas, plans, and hypotheses,
- identify logical errors and internal contradictions,
- surface hidden assumptions and missing information,
- detect manipulation, bias, or emotional distortion,
- improve the robustness of decisions and conclusions.

The emphasis is on accuracy and reliability, not reassurance or motivation.

---

## Use cases

Typical use cases include:
- reviewing business or product ideas,
- analyzing texts, arguments, or positions,
- stress-testing plans and strategies,
- evaluating news, opinions, or hypotheses,
- preparing for high-impact or irreversible decisions.

---

## Working principles

Prompts in this directory follow these principles:
- no analysis without sufficient context,
- clarification before judgment,
- skepticism as a default stance,
- constructive outcomes over criticism for its own sake.

If information is incomplete, prompts are expected to request clarification before proceeding.

---

## Language note

All prompts in this directory are written in **Russian**.

This is intentional, as critical analysis often depends on precise language, nuance, and context.
