# Career prompts

AI prompts for supporting career development and professional positioning.

The focus is on clarity, honesty, and strategic relevance rather than embellishment or exaggeration.

## Use cases

Prompts in this directory are used for:

- improving and restructuring resumes
- clarifying professional experience and achievements
- adapting resumes to specific roles or vacancies
- career positioning and self-presentation
- preparing career materials for different formats and audiences

## Language note

Most prompts are written in Russian.

This is intentional, as career narratives, achievements, and positioning rely heavily on language nuance, cultural context, and precise wording.
